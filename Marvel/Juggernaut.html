Biography

Cain Marko, better known as Juggernaut, is a long time enemy and sometimes ally of the X-Men. As a child, Cain was regularly abused by his alcoholic father. His stepbrother, Charles Xavier, on the other hand was treated much better while also exceeding him in studies and sports. All of it caused Cain to hate Xavier for years. While serving in the US army, Cain accidently found the Temple of Cyttorak and obtained the Crimson Gem. The Gem's mystical power transformed Marko into Cyttorak's avatar on Earth â€” the Juggernaut. Marko used his powers to spread chaos and cause destruction often together with his partner-in-crime Black Tom Cassidy. After years of being a villain and terrorist, Marko started to reform. He reconciled with Xavier, joined the X-Men and later the Excalibur. When Asgardian God of Fear descended on Earth, Cain was possessed by one of its servants, Kuurth Breaker of Stone, for a short time. Eventually, Marko became Cyttorak's avatar once again and gained even greater strength.
